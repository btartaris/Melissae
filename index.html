From Hive to Home

The Melissae method

01

Terroir Selection

We identify exceptional ecosystems — remote valleys, altitude meadows, ancient groves — where bees forage untouched flora.

02

Artisan Harvest

Partner beekeepers harvest by hand at peak bloom, leaving enough honey for the colony's needs. Ethical, always.

03

Cold Extraction

Frames are uncapped gently and spun cold. No heat, no filtration — every enzyme and antioxidant preserved.

04

Sealed with Care

Bottled in dark glass to protect bioactivity. Each jar hand-labelled with harvest date, beekeeper, and origin coordinates.
